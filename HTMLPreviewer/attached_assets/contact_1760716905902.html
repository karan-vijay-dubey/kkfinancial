Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How long does the loan approval process take?

The approval time varies by loan type and bank. Personal loans can be approved within 24-48 hours, while housing loans typically take 7-15 days. We ensure the fastest possible processing through our direct bank relationships.

What documents are required for loan application?

Basic documents include identity proof, address proof, income documents, and bank statements. Specific requirements vary by loan type. We provide a detailed checklist during consultation and help with document preparation.

Do you charge any fees for consultation?

No, our consultation services are completely free. We don't charge any upfront fees. Our revenue comes from the banks after successful loan disbursement, so our success is tied to yours.

Can you help improve my CIBIL score?

Yes, we provide free CIBIL consultation and guidance on improving your credit score. We analyze your credit report and suggest actionable steps to enhance your creditworthiness for better loan terms.

Which banks do you work with?

We have direct partnerships with over 15 leading banks and NBFCs including SBI, HDFC, ICICI, Axis Bank, Kotak Mahindra, and many more. This allows us to offer you the best rates and terms available in the market.

Can I apply for loans even with low CIBIL score?

Yes, we work with multiple lenders who offer loans to applicants with low CIBIL scores. We help identify the right lender based on your profile and work on strategies to improve your eligibility.
